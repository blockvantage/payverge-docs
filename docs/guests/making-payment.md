## Paying with Crypto (USDC)

### Step-by-Step Process

#### 1. Select Crypto Payment

1. **Tap "Pay with Crypto"**
2. **Review Amount** - Total in USDC
3. **Check Gas Fees** - Small network fee
4. **Confirm** - Proceed to wallet

TODO: ADD IMAGE - Screenshot of crypto payment selection

#### 2. Connect Your Wallet

**First Time:**
1. **Choose Wallet** - MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.
2. **Approve Connection** - In your wallet app
3. **Sign Message** - Verify ownership
4. **Connected** - Ready to pay

**Returning:**
- Wallet remembers connection
- One-tap to reconnect
- Faster checkout

TODO: ADD IMAGE - Screenshot of wallet connection options

#### 3. Approve USDC Spending

**One-Time Setup:**
1. **Approve Transaction** - In wallet
2. **Set Allowance** - Amount you authorize
3. **Confirm** - Sign transaction
4. **Wait** - 10-30 seconds for confirmation

**Why This Step?**
This allows the restaurant to receive USDC from your wallet. You only do this once per restaurant.

#### 4. Complete Payment

1. **Review Final Amount**
2. **Confirm Payment** - In wallet
3. **Sign Transaction**
4. **Wait for Confirmation** - 10-30 seconds
5. **Payment Complete!** - Receipt generated

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Failed

**Common Reasons:**
- Insufficient funds
- Card declined
- Network error
- Wallet not connected
- Gas fee too low

**Solutions:**
1. Check your balance
2. Verify card details
3. Try different payment method
4. Check internet connection
5. Contact your bank/wallet

---

### Crypto Payment Issues

#### Wallet Won't Connect

**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page
- Try different wallet
- Check wallet is unlocked
- Update wallet app
- Clear browser cache

#### Not Enough USDC

**Solutions:**
- Buy USDC on exchange
- Transfer from another wallet
- Use different payment method
- Ask friend to pay (split later)

#### Gas Fees Too High

**Solutions:**
- Wait for lower fees
- Increase gas limit
- Use different network
- Try traditional payment

#### Transaction Stuck

**Solutions:**
- Wait 5-10 minutes
- Check wallet for pending
- Speed up transaction (if option available)
- Contact support with transaction hash

---

### Card Payment Issues

#### Card Declined

**Reasons:**
- Insufficient funds
- Card expired
- Wrong CVV
- Bank security hold
- International transaction blocked

**Solutions:**
- Check card balance
- Verify expiration date
- Confirm CVV code
- Call your bank
- Try different card

#### Payment Processing Error

**Solutions:**
- Retry payment
- Check internet connection
- Try different browser
- Clear cookies
- Use different payment method

### PayPal Issues

**Solutions:**

**Solutions:**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is it safe to pay with crypto?
Yes! Blockchain transactions are secure and transparent. Your payment is verified on-chain.

### Can I get a refund?
Refunds depend on restaurant policy. Contact the restaurant directly for refund requests.

### Do I need a crypto wallet?
Only if paying with crypto. Traditional payment methods don't require a wallet.

### What if I overpay?
Contact the restaurant immediately. They can process a refund for the difference.

### Can I pay cash?
Some restaurants accept cash at the counter. Check with your server.

### Is there a payment limit?
Limits vary by payment method and restaurant. Large bills may require special handling.

### What about payment disputes?
Contact the restaurant first. For crypto payments, transactions are final and cannot be reversed.

### Can I split payment methods?
Currently, each person must use one payment method. You can split the bill among multiple people.
