## Subscription Management

### Viewing Your Subscription

Access subscription details from your dashboard:

1. Go to **Business Dashboard**
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Select **Subscription** section

You'll see:
- Current plan (Free or Pro)
- Subscription status (Active, Expired, Expiring Soon)
- Renewal date
- Payment method
- Transaction history

### Renewing Your Subscription

#### Automatic Renewal (Credit Card)
If you pay with credit card, your subscription renews automatically each month.

#### Manual Renewal (Crypto)
If you pay with crypto, you'll need to manually renew:

1. You'll receive reminder emails 7 days before expiration
2. Dashboard shows "Expiring Soon" warning
3. Click **"Renew Subscription"**
4. Choose payment method
5. Complete payment

**Grace Period:** You have 3 days after expiration to renew without losing data.

---

## Subscription Status

### Active
Your subscription is current and all features are available.

### Expiring Soon
Your subscription expires in less than 7 days. Renew to maintain access.

### Expired
Your subscription has expired. You have 3 days to renew before features are restricted.

### Grace Period
You're in the 3-day grace period after expiration. Renew now to avoid restrictions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I switch payment methods?
Yes! You can change from crypto to card or vice versa at renewal time.

### What happens if my payment fails?
You'll receive an email notification. Update your payment method and try again. You have a 3-day grace period.

### Do you offer annual plans?
Yes! Annual crypto payments receive ~2 months free (pay for 10, get 12).

### Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled case-by-case. Contact support@payverge.io to discuss.

### What if I need more than Pro tier features?
Contact us for enterprise solutions with custom features and pricing.

### Is there a setup fee?
Only the one-time blockchain registration fee during initial signup.

### Can I pause my subscription?
Not currently, but you can cancel and reactivate anytime without losing data.
