# Business Registration

This guide walks you through creating your Payverge business account step-by-step.

- ‚úÖ A crypto wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, or similar)

## Step 2: Business Information

Fill in your basic business details:

### Business Name
- Enter your restaurant or business name
- This will appear on guest menus and receipts
- Example: "The Golden Fork Restaurant"

### Business Description
- Brief description of your business (optional)
- Helps guests know what to expect
- Example: "Modern Italian cuisine with a cozy atmosphere"

### Contact Information
- **Email:** Primary contact email for your business
- **Phone:** Business phone number (with country code)
- **Website:** Your business website (optional)

### Physical Address
- **Street Address:** Full street address
- **City:** City name
- **State/Province:** State or province
- **Postal Code:** ZIP or postal code
- **Country:** Select your country

## Step 3: Business Logo

Upload your business logo to personalize your guest experience.

### Logo Requirements
- **Format:** JPG, PNG, or SVG
- **Size:** Maximum 2MB
- **Recommended:** Square format (1:1 ratio)
- **Dimensions:** At least 500x500 pixels for best quality

### How to Upload
1. Click **"Upload Logo"** or drag and drop your file
2. Preview your logo
3. Adjust if needed

**Don't have a logo?** You can skip this step and add it later from Business Settings.

### Connecting Your Wallet

1. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
2. Choose your wallet provider:
   - MetaMask
   - WalletConnect
   - Coinbase Wallet
   - Other compatible wallets
3. Approve the connection in your wallet

When you connect your wallet:
- ‚úÖ Your wallet address becomes your business identifier on the blockchain

- ‚úÖ You authenticate securely without passwords
- ‚ùå We never have access to your funds or private keys

## Step 5: Blockchain Registration

After connecting your wallet, your business is registered on the blockchain.

### Registration Fee

There's a one-time registration fee to create your business on the blockchain:
- **Fee:** Typically 10-25 USDC (varies by network)
- **Purpose:** Covers blockchain transaction costs
- **One-time:** You only pay this once

### Registration Process

1. Review the registration fee
2. Click **"Register Business"**
3. Approve the USDC spending in your wallet
4. Confirm the registration transaction
5. Wait for blockchain confirmation (usually 10-30 seconds)

:::tip What's Happening?

### Registration Steps Explained

You'll see a progress indicator showing:
1. **Approval** (25%) - Authorizing USDC payment
2. **Registration** (50%) - Creating your blockchain business profile
3. **Backend** (75%) - Setting up your dashboard and features
4. **Upload** (90%) - Processing your logo and assets
5. **Complete** (100%) - Your business is ready!

**Important:** Don't close the page during registration. The process takes 30-60 seconds.

## Step 6: Choose Your Subscription

Select a subscription plan that fits your business needs.

### Available Plans

#### Free Tier
- **Cost:** $0/month
- **Features:**
  - Up to 5 tables
  - Basic menu (up to 20 items)
  - Standard support
  - Crypto payments only
- **Best for:** Testing the platform or very small operations

#### Pro Tier
- **Cost:** $29/month (or equivalent in USDC)
- **Features:**
  - Unlimited tables
  - Unlimited menu items
  - Staff management (unlimited staff)
  - Kitchen display system
  - Advanced analytics
  - Multi-language support
  - Multi-currency display
  - Priority support
  - All payment methods
- **Best for:** Active restaurants and businesses

### Payment Options

**Option 1: Crypto (USDC)**
- Pay directly with USDC from your wallet
- Instant activation
- No processing fees

**Option 2: Credit Card (via Stripe)**
- Pay with any major credit/debit card
- Standard card processing
- Automatic monthly billing

### Referral Codes & Discounts

Have a referral code or coupon? Enter it during checkout to receive:
- Discounted registration fees
- Reduced subscription costs
- Special promotional offers

## Troubleshooting Registration

### Wallet Won't Connect
- Ensure you have a compatible wallet installed
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser

### Transaction Failed
- Check you have enough USDC in your wallet
- Ensure you have ETH/native token for gas fees
- Try increasing gas price if network is congested
- Contact support if issue persists

### Registration Stuck
- Don't refresh the page during registration
- Wait at least 2 minutes for blockchain confirmation
- Check your wallet for pending transactions
- Contact support with your wallet address if stuck

### Can't Upload Logo
- Ensure file is under 2MB
- Use JPG, PNG, or SVG format
- Try a different image
- You can add logo later from Settings
