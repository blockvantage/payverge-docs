# Understanding User Roles

Payverge has three main types of users, each with different capabilities and access levels. Understanding these roles will help you make the most of the platform.

## ğŸ¢ Business Owners

Business owners are the primary administrators of a Payverge account. They have full access to all features and settings.

### What Business Owners Can Do

- **Complete Control** - Access all features and settings
- **Financial Management** - View revenue, manage subscriptions, and handle payments
- **Business Settings** - Configure branding, hours, and business information
- **Menu Management** - Create and edit menus, categories, and items
- **Table Management** - Create tables and generate QR codes
- **Staff Management** - Invite staff members and assign roles
- **Bill Management** - Create, view, and close bills
- **Analytics Access** - View detailed reports and insights
- **Plugin Configuration** - Enable and configure payment methods and integrations

### How to Become a Business Owner

1. Visit the Payverge website
2. Click "Start Your Business"
3. Complete the registration process
4. Connect your crypto wallet (for blockchain registration)
5. Choose and pay for a subscription plan

**Note:** Business owners authenticate using their crypto wallet (Sign-In with Ethereum/SIWE).

## ğŸ½ï¸ Guests (Customers)

Guests are your customers who visit your establishment and use Payverge to browse menus, order, and pay.

### What Guests Can Do

- **Scan QR Codes** - Access your menu by scanning the table QR code
- **Browse Menu** - View items with photos, descriptions, and prices
- **Customize Orders** - Select options and add special requests
- **Place Orders** - Submit orders directly to your kitchen
- **View Bills** - See itemized bills in real-time
- **Split Bills** - Divide bills among multiple people
- **Make Payments** - Pay with crypto or other enabled payment methods
- **View Receipts** - Access digital receipts after payment

### Guest Experience Flow

1. **Arrive** - Guest sits at a table
2. **Scan** - Scans the QR code on the table
3. **Browse** - Views your digital menu on their phone
4. **Order** - Selects items and places an order
5. **Track** - Sees order status (pending, approved, preparing, ready)
6. **Pay** - Views bill and completes payment
7. **Leave** - Receives digital receipt

**Note:** Guests don't need to create accounts or download apps - everything works through their mobile browser!

## Authentication Methods

### Business Owners
- **Method:** Sign-In with Ethereum (SIWE)
- **Requirement:** Crypto wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
- **Security:** Blockchain-based authentication
- **Why:** Enables blockchain registration and crypto payments

### Staff Members
- **Method:** Email + One-Time Codes
- **Requirement:** Email address only
- **Security:** 6-digit verification codes sent via email
- **Why:** Simple access without crypto knowledge

### Guests
- **Method:** No authentication required
- **Requirement:** Just scan the QR code
- **Security:** Session-based access to specific table
- **Why:** Fastest, most convenient experience

## Choosing the Right Role

When inviting team members, consider their responsibilities:

| If they need to... | Assign role... |
|-------------------|----------------|
| Manage the entire restaurant | **Manager** |
| Take orders and handle payments | **Server** |
| Seat guests and manage tables | **Host** |
| Prepare food and track orders | **Kitchen** |

You can always change a staff member's role later if their responsibilities change.
