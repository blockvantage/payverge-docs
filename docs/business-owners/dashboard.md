# Business Dashboard Overview

Your business dashboard is the command center for managing your Payverge operations. This guide will help you understand and navigate all the features available to you.

## Accessing Your Dashboard

1. Visit [payverge.io](https://payverge.io)
2. Click **"Business Dashboard"**
3. Connect your wallet to sign in
4. You'll land on your dashboard home page

## Dashboard Home

The dashboard home page gives you a quick overview of your business operations.

### Live Metrics

At the top of your dashboard, you'll see real-time statistics:

#### Active Bills
- Number of currently open bills
- Click to view all active bills
- Updates in real-time as bills are created/closed

#### Total Tables
- Total number of tables in your system
- Shows active vs inactive tables
- Quick link to table management

#### Today's Sales
- Total revenue for the current day
- Resets at midnight
- Includes all payment methods

#### Kitchen Status
- Number of orders ready for pickup/delivery
- Helps track kitchen efficiency
- Links to kitchen display

## Navigation Tips

### Sidebar Navigation

The left sidebar is your main navigation tool:

- **Always Visible** - Stays open on desktop
- **Collapsible** - Can be hidden for more space
- **Mobile Friendly** - Converts to hamburger menu on mobile
- **Active Indicators** - Current page highlighted

### Breadcrumbs

Track your location in the dashboard:
- Shows current section and page
- Click any breadcrumb to navigate back
- Helpful for deep navigation

### Search Function

Quickly find what you need:
- Press `/` to open search
- Search for features, settings, or help
- Keyboard shortcuts for power users

## Troubleshooting

### Dashboard Won't Load
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Ensure wallet is connected

### Real-time Updates Not Working
- Refresh the page
- Check WebSocket connection
- Disable browser extensions
- Contact support if persistent

### Can't Access Certain Features
- Verify your subscription tier
- Check your user role
- Ensure feature is enabled
- Review plugin status
